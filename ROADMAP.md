# IELC-CRM Project Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the development phases for the IELC-CRM system, focusing on a seamless flow from Lead Acquisition to Invoice Generation and Student Enrolment.

---

## 🚀 Vision
To build a premium, automated, and efficient CRM and Academic management system for IELC, ensuring all leads are followed up, assessed accurately, and enrolled smoothly.

---

## 🛤️ Phase 1: Lead Acquisition & Qualification (Current)
*Focus: Capturing leads and managing the initial follow-up pipeline.*

- [x] **Lead Join Form (Public)**: Responsive form for prospective students to register.
- [x] **Admin Registration Inbox**: Approval/Rejection interface for incoming leads.
- [x] **CRM Dashboard & Kanban**: Visual representation of the lead pipeline across branches.
- [/] **WhatsApp Integration (Baileys)**: Real-time messaging for follow-ups and automated template sending.
- [ ] **Lead Activities & Follow-up Logs**: Tracking every interaction with a lead for accountability.

## 🎓 Phase 2: Academic Assessment
*Focus: Measuring student level to provide the right course recommendation.*

- [x] **Placement Test (PT) Engine**: Automated online testing platform.
- [/] **Manual Grading UI**: Interface for teachers to grade subjective parts of the PT.
- [ ] **PT Session Management**: Tracking when leads take the test and their historical results.


## 💰 Phase 3: Sales Conversion & Price Mastering
*Focus: Turning a qualified lead into a customer.*

- [x] **Price Master Data**: Centralized settings for tuition fees, registration, and material costs.
- [x] **Consultation Logs**: Recording parents/students feedback and specific needs.
- [/] **Class Plotting**: Assigning a lead to a specific study class before invoicing.
- [ ] **Discount & Scholarship Logic**: Handling special pricing scenarios.

## 📝 Phase 4: Finance & Invoicing
*Focus: Professional billing and payment verification.*

- [/] **Invoice Generation Engine**: Creating detailed invoices from lead/class data.
- [/] **PDF Streaming/Download**: Clean, professional branded invoice PDF for parents.


## 🎒 Phase 5: Student Lifecycle & Academic Operations
*Focus: Managing the active student journey.*

- [x] **Student Promotion**: Automatically converting a Lead to a Student upon invoice payment.
- [ ] **Re-registration (Renewal)**: Facilitating the next payment cycle for existing students.
